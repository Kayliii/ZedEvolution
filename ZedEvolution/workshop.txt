version=1
title=Evolution
description=[h1]Evolution Mod[/h1]
description=Allows zombies to evolve various attributes over time.
description=
description=Make zombies change their speed, strength, toughness, cognition, ability to crawl under cars, memory, sight, hearing, and virus transmission as a function of time, using individually adjustable settings in the sandbox options.
description=
description=[h2]Customization[/h2]
description=[h3]General Settings[/h3]
description=[list]
description=[*]Turn evolution on or off.
description=[*]Set how many days you have before evolutions begins. A negative value means evolution has already begun.
description=[*]Set how evolved zombies are from the start. A negative value means zombies start "devolved".
description=[/list]
description=
description=[h3]Evolution Function[/h3]
description=[list]
description=[*]Set the way in which evolution progresses to be either linear, asymptotic or cyclic.
description=[*]For a linear function, you can specify how long it takes to reach 100% evolution.
description=[*]For an asymptotic function, you can specify the base value, the limit, and how long it takes to reach 50% evolution.
description=[*]For a cyclic function, you can specify how often it repeats, and between which numbers evolution fluctuates.
description=[/list]
description=
description=[h3]Evolution Limits[/h3]
description=[list]
description=[*]Set how strong or weak zombies can get compared to the zombie lore settings.
description=[*]Transmission is hard capped at "Blood + Saliva" and cannot become "Everyone's Infected".
description=[/list]
description=
description=[h3]Evolution Factor[/h3]
description=[list]
description=[*]Set how quickly zombies evolve certain attributes compared to others.
description=[*]Set the factor to a negative number to make zombies weaker.
description=[*]Set the factor to 0 to stop zombies from evolving this attribute.
description=[/list]
description=
description=[h3]Deviation Factor[/h3]
description=[list]
description=[*]Set how evolution of certain attributes is distributed across the zombie population.
description=[*]Half of zombies will be affected less than the given percentage, and half will be affected more.
description=[*]As the percentage approaches 0% or 100%, zombies become more similar.
description=[*]As the percentage approaches 50%, zombies become more diverse.
description=[/list]
description=
description=[h3]Zombie Lore[/h3]
description=[list]
description=[*]Any attributes set to "Random" are unaffected by evolution.
description=[*]Transmission is unaffected by evolution if set to "Everyone's Infected".
description=[/list]
description=
